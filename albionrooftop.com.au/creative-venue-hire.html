Venue Hire

Looking for a dynamic, visually striking location in the heart of South Melbourne?

The Albion Rooftop is available for hire — ideal for photo shoots, podcast recordings, video content, brand campaigns and more. With its refined aesthetic, natural light, and open-air atmosphere, the rooftop provides an exceptional backdrop for creatives, brands and production teams alike.
